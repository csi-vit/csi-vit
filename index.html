ABOUT US

The Computer Society Of India is a non-profit professional body that meets to exchange views and information to learn and share ideas. Being a national level committee, we work together to discuss technology with likeminded prople. CSI is known for conducting a pleothra of events ranging from seminars, workshops, games and intercations with proffesionals from industry. These events aim to create a conductive enviorment for geeks and computer entusiasts by hosting events and other fun activitesto promote learning and contribute to real life skills of attendes. CSI aims to infuse education with fun to bring the process of learning to a culmination and to light all bdding engineers with a passion to push their skills beyond limits.
